Trend-Range-Confluence (TRC)

Unveil our highly unique, completely mechanical trading framework. Designed to pinpoint low-risk, explosive-growth turnarounds by synthesizing institutional vectors.

  • Trend Alignment (T): Identifying master institutional directions using premium macro-level flows to ensure trading on the side of global bank algorithms.
  • Range Equilibrium (R): Isolating active dealing ranges. Strictly limiting entries to steep discount areas for buys and premium areas for shorts.
  • Confluence Confirmation (C): Lower timeframe execution triggers backed by structural liquidity hunts, FVG closures, and order block mitigations.
  • Rules-Based Invalidation: Standardized mathematics establishing exact, emotionless parameters for exit points and stop placement.
